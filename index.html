    Kate's Fish Camp

    Located at the cross roads of SR 20, Prairie Creek and The Gainesville Bike trail, Kates Fish Camp makes an ideal place for outdoor adventures, canoeing, boating, camping, biking, bird watching and more.

    Amenities

    Kate's Fish Camp is more than just a place to park your RV, it is an exclusive campground completely surrounded by extraordinary wilderness with many trails to explore. Our campground is directly connected to the Gainesville-Hawthorne State Bike Trail and has total access to Newnan's Lake. All of our sites are pull-through sites, and each comes with electricity (20, 30, and 50 amp), water, and Gigablast high-speed Wi-Fi.

    Note: All campers must be self-contained, and only RV camping is available, although we offer a guest restroom and a shower for your use as Kate's Fish Camp does not offer sewage hookups because of the surrounding land's recognized natural, ecological and cultural values. There is a Love's Travel Stops about 15 minutes away that offers RV septic services.
