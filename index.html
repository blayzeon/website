    Conveniently located along Prairie Creek, Newnans Lake and the Gainesville-Hawthorne State Trail, Kates Fish Camp makes an ideal place for outdoor adventures, canoeing, boating, camping, biking, bird watching and more.

    Amenities

    All sites include:

    • Pull-through access
    • Electricity (20, 30, and 50 amp)
    • Water hookups

    Campground amenitites include:

    • High-speed Wi-Fi
    • Guest bathroom
    • Outdoor Shower
    • Outdoor grills
    • Picnic tables

    Local Amenities:

    • Direct trail access
    • Direct access to Newnans Lake and Prairie Creek for fishing and boating
    • Located 15-minutes away from the heart of Gainesville, including UF and Shands, and more.

    Prices and Availabity

    Pricing is for RVs, travel trailers, and pop-up campers. We do not accommodate camping for other units such as tents or vans.

    • Class B Recreational Vehicles or Travel Trailers less than 25' are $40.00
    • Class C Recreational Vehicles or Travel Trailers less than 30' are $45.00
    • Class A Recreational Vehicles or Travel Trailers more than 30' are $50.00
    • We only offer nightly rates. Campers/RVs can have up to four adults; additional adults are $10 per person.

    We allow stays of up to 2 weeks. To book your site, please give us a call at (352) 372-1026 or use the contact form below.

    All cancellations and rain checks must be made five workdays before your reservation.

    Happy Camper Rules

    • Quiet hours are between 10 PM and 7 AM
    • No loud music, barking dogs, or disturbing other campers allowed
    • No dogs tied up outside or left unattended
    • You are welcome to make campfires, firewood is available, but no cutting standing green trees or burning trash
